STRATEGY_NAME: Classic Double Bottom
STRATEGY_ID: classic_double_bottom
VERSION: 1.0
AUTHOR: Ovi
MARKET: crypto, forex, indices, commodities
DIRECTION: long
HTF: 1d
MTF: 4h
LTF: 15m

CONCEPT:
A Double Bottom is a bullish reversal pattern where price tests the same
support level twice and fails to break lower both times. It indicates
that sellers are losing strength at that price level and buyers are
stepping in with conviction.

CONDITION_1: Two distinct lows at approximately the same price level (within 1.5%)
CONDITION_2: A clear peak between the two lows (neckline)
CONDITION_3: Price approaching or breaking the neckline on the second bottom
CONDITION_4: Volume ideally higher on the second bottom or neckline break

ENTRY_TRIGGER: Breakout and close above the neckline (peak between the two lows)
ENTRY_TYPE: breakout or limit on retest
STOP_PLACEMENT: Below the lower of the two bottoms with 0.2% buffer
STOP_TYPE: structure_based
TARGET_1: 50% at measured move (neckline + distance from bottom to neckline)
TARGET_2: Previous significant resistance
MINIMUM_RR: 2.0

INVALIDATION_1: Price closes below either bottom
INVALIDATION_2: Neckline breaks but immediately reverses back below

DETECT_STEP_1: Find swing lows in last 40 candles
DETECT_STEP_2: Identify two lows within 1.5% of each other
DETECT_STEP_3: Find the peak between them (neckline)
DETECT_STEP_4: Check if current price is near or above neckline
DETECT_STEP_5: Calculate measured move target
