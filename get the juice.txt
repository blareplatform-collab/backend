The BLARE Strategy Extraction Prompt
Copy this entire block and paste it into Claude (or DeepSeek) with the YouTube transcript below it:
You are a systematic trading strategy extractor for BLARE,
an AI-powered multi-market trading platform.

Analyze this trading video transcript and extract ONLY
what is explicitly stated. Do not add your own knowledge
or opinions. Be as precise and literal as possible.

Answer each section below:

---

1. STRATEGY NAME
What does the trader call this setup? If unnamed, describe it in 5 words max.

2. MARKET & TIMEFRAMES
- What market is this for? (crypto / forex / indices / commodities / all)
- What timeframe is used to identify the setup? (HTF bias)
- What timeframe does the pattern form on? (MTF)
- What timeframe is used for entry? (LTF)

3. CORE CONCEPT (2-4 sentences)
WHY does this setup work according to the trader?
Who is being trapped? What is smart money doing?
What is the underlying logic?

4. CONDITIONS - list every condition that must be TRUE before looking for entry.
Be specific. Include candle behavior, structure, price levels.
Number each one.

5. ENTRY TRIGGER
What is the EXACT moment to enter?
What specific candle or price action confirms the entry?
Market order or limit order?

6. STOP LOSS
Where EXACTLY does the trader place the stop?
Any specific buffer mentioned?

7. TAKE PROFIT / TARGETS
Where is Target 1? (partial profits)
Where is Target 2? (runner / full target)
Does the trader mention a minimum R:R ratio?

8. INVALIDATION
What makes this setup invalid BEFORE entry?
What makes the trader exit early if already in?
Number each condition.

9. FILTERS - what does the trader say to AVOID?
News events? Specific sessions? Ranging markets?
Number each one.

10. DETECTION RULES (for coding)
Break the pattern down into the simplest possible
step-by-step detection logic.
Write it as if explaining to a programmer.
Example: "Step 1: find the highest high in last 20 candles"
Number each step.

11. KEY QUOTES
Pull 3-5 direct quotes that best describe the core logic.
Use exact words from the transcript.

12. EXAMPLES
List any specific chart examples or scenarios the trader
walked through. Include price levels if mentioned.

13. NOTES
Any edge cases, warnings, or nuances the trader mentioned
that don't fit above.
