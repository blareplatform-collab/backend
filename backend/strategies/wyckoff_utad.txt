STRATEGY_NAME: Wyckoff UTAD Distribution
STRATEGY_ID: wyckoff_utad
VERSION: 1.0
AUTHOR: Ovi
MARKET: crypto, forex, indices
DIRECTION: short
HTF: 1d
MTF: 4h
LTF: 15m

CONCEPT:
UTAD (Upthrust After Distribution) is the mirror of the Spring.
In Wyckoff distribution, the UTAD is a false breakout above range resistance
designed to trap late buyers before the markdown phase begins.

CONDITION_1: Price in defined range for at least 20+ candles on MTF
CONDITION_2: Multiple tests of both range high and range low (at least 2 each)
CONDITION_3: Price briefly breaks above range resistance (UTAD)
CONDITION_4: Price quickly falls back inside the range within 1-3 candles

ENTRY_TRIGGER: Close back below range resistance after the UTAD wick
ENTRY_TYPE: market
STOP_PLACEMENT: Above the UTAD high with 0.1% buffer
STOP_TYPE: structure_based
TARGET_1: 50% at range midpoint
TARGET_2: Range low and below
MINIMUM_RR: 2.0

INVALIDATION_1: Price holds above range resistance and continues up
INVALIDATION_2: Range expands significantly after the UTAD

DETECT_STEP_1: Find 60-candle range with touches_high >= 2 and touches_low >= 2
DETECT_STEP_2: Find candle that wicks above range_high * 1.001
DETECT_STEP_3: Check next 1-3 candles close back below range_high
DETECT_STEP_4: Entry on the rejection close
