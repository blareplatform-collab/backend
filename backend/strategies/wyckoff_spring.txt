STRATEGY_NAME: Wyckoff Spring Entry
STRATEGY_ID: wyckoff_spring
VERSION: 1.0
AUTHOR: Ovi
MARKET: crypto, forex, indices
DIRECTION: long
HTF: 1d
MTF: 4h
LTF: 15m

CONCEPT:
In Wyckoff accumulation, the Spring is a false breakdown below the range support
designed to shake out weak hands before the markup phase. It is one of the highest
probability long setups in all of technical analysis.

CONDITION_1: Price in defined range for at least 20+ candles on MTF
CONDITION_2: Multiple tests of both range high and range low (at least 2 each)
CONDITION_3: Price briefly breaks below range support (Spring)
CONDITION_4: Price quickly recovers back inside the range within 1-3 candles

ENTRY_TRIGGER: Close back above range support after the Spring wick
ENTRY_TYPE: market
STOP_PLACEMENT: Below the Spring low with 0.1% buffer
STOP_TYPE: structure_based
TARGET_1: 50% at range midpoint
TARGET_2: Range high and beyond
MINIMUM_RR: 2.0

INVALIDATION_1: Price closes below Spring low and continues down
INVALIDATION_2: Range expands significantly after the Spring

DETECT_STEP_1: Find 60-candle range with touches_high >= 2 and touches_low >= 2
DETECT_STEP_2: Find candle that wicks below range_low * 0.999
DETECT_STEP_3: Check next 1-3 candles close back above range_low
DETECT_STEP_4: Entry on the recovery close
